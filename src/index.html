PATHFINDER

The only limits to our realization of tomorrow will be our doubts of today We see a lot of young minds confused and concerned about their future. "What after college?" Has this thought crossed your mind anytime? If it has and you are yet to find a solution, then dear CEAns, we've got this covered. The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centre(IEDC)-CEA unit is organising Pathfinder- an extensive talk on various career options after B.Tech . This will be conducted at the College auditorium on 19th September 2019 Thursday from 10:30 am to 12:30 pm.

About the speaker:

Allen Thomas Kannattu is an established career guide, Influencer and marketer who heads Career Launcher, Thiruvalla. He is also the Director of Kannattu Arun Group, a well established business firm in the state.

We whole-heartedly welcome all dear CEAns to this event.
